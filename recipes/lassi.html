Lassi Recipe

Description

Lassi originated in the Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent. The word lassi means yogurt mixed with water in Punjabi.

It is sweet and have a creamy texture and a wonderful accompaniment for a spicy Indian or even Mexican meal.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chopped mint leaves
  • Chopped dry fruits.
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar

Steps

  • Combine yogurt, water and milk in a blender.
  • Add black pepper, mint, cumin and sugar to taste.
  • Blend the mixture untill smooth.
  • Add dry fruits to the blended mixture.
  • Serve plain or over ice garnished with dry fruits from top.
